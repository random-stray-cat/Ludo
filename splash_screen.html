RULES

On your turn roll 2d6. If it lands on a pair one of your four pawns can leave home, progress and you can throw again. The 2 die numbers are played as clock-wise progress by one or 2 of your pawns. If you land on an opponent's pawn, that pawn goes back home. A pawn's last turn walking in the home tower in the center of the board must be no more than the exact number of steps remaining. Collect all 4 of your pawns in the home tower of your colour before your opponent.
